A Unit refers to a singular instance of a character that can be used within the game. Units are primarily obtainable through Summon, in which you exchange various currencies for a chance at certain units. During a Stage, a unit's primary use is to protect the player's base by attacking enemies on the track. To do this, units typically operate on an attack cycle built on an SPA (Second Per Attack) stat and a Range (the effective area a unit can target in) stat. Additionally, every attacking unit has a Damage stat that usually pertains to the damage of their attacks.

Not every unit attacks; some units are responsible for alternative mechanics that do not involve attacking, such as generating Yen or applying Buffs to other units. Additionally certain units may operate absent of any number of their stats; for example, a unit may attack without regarding its range.

Units are often categorized with greater types (Rarity, Elements, Archetypes) and may be further sorted through more specific mechanics.

Players are given multiple ways to strengthen their Units.

  • Stat Values are permanent values assigned to a unit that apply a percentage change to a unit's base stat value. Units come with random Stat Values and the player may reroll them.
  • Traits are permanent enhancements to a unit that apply a large variety of effects to a singular copy of a unit. At the cost of Trait Rerolls, a Trait can be rolled & then rerolled.
  • Equipment are equippable items that have built in stat values & passives for the enhancement of the equipping unit. Certain units have special, rarer equipment that may alter and/or add to their passives.
